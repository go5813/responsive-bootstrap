The Saga

George was born and raised in Kenya where he became an expert at picking his own switch from the bush, soap bar sculpting, and a brilliant bass singer in a boys choir. He also swam in one of Africa's great lakes as he bathed 'just because he could!'.

Being the youngest of 15, this tall stick figured man, never cracked a smile despite his warm enterpreneurial spirit. At the age of 20, George put his Kenyan running roots to full throttle and booked a flight to Atlanta GA USA. Now this dandy enjoys testing his flat footed chicken legs along the Atlanta belt line.

In 2013 he married his green eyed, red haired, fair skinned chai latte. George is living his happy ever after somewhere in the burrows of Atlanta with their 4 kids "3 poodles and a kerry blue terrier".
